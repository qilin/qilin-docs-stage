    Help customers quickly search your game

    Super!Store uses tags, genres and features to make customers relevant search. It will be listed in the navigation menu above the name of your product, also in filters. Depending on it, Super!Store shows your game in the similar games block.

    Tags

    Select the most applicable meanings to your game. Top 10 tags will help customers find your game from a mass of similar genres.

    Requirements:

    • At least 1 tag, up to 10 tags.
