    Descriptions and links

    Descriptions and links

    Describe your game with an impressive tagline and description. The descriptions help you stand out from the mass of games.

    Tagline

    A tagline is displayed on a store page. The tagline provides a summary of a web page. Search engines show this page description in search results.

    It’s not a technical limit, but you can take into account, that the description length depends on the display width, search engine and others. Keep your tagline between 160–300 characters.

    Be sure, the one who likes the short description will not miss your game. If you catch people with a tagline they will go deeper to the full description.

    Go to your game’s store page on Descriptions tab > Tagline to fill in a brief awesome tagline.

    Description

    Do you imagine how to create an awesome game description?

    Follow a common structure:

    1. The first essential sentence

    Draw the attention of your customers in the first sentence with the main idea of your game.

    2. A few descriptive sentences

    Describe the current atmosphere or situations, the main characters, their purposes and intentions, instead of a rough long description.

    Go to your game’s store page on Descriptions tab > Description to fill in an impressive description.

    Links

    Provide external links for your customers to get to know you, to obtain support and communication:

    • Your game website
    • Facebook
    • Twitter
    • YouTube
    • Twitch
    • Discord
    • VKontakte
    • Reddit

    Go to your game’s store page on Descriptions tab > External links to fill in the links.
