    General Settings

    Media

    A quality game matters the most but screenshots play an important role in the success of your game too.

    Your game images can be displayed on all devices including 4K and Retina displays. After uploading images, we will automatically resize it for Full HD and other displays.

    Cover image

    The cover image is used as a special featuring block on the store home page at the very top of the page.

    This image is the first impression the game gives your customers.

    The default language is English, and you can also specify other languages for the cover image.

    Requirements:

    • Size: 2160x1215 px (aspect ratio 1.77)

    • Format: PNG, JPEG

    Game video

    The YouTube video is used on the game’s store page in the screenshot carousel. The very first video can be used in featuring blocks on the store main page.

    The default language is English, and you can also specify the YouTube links for other languages.

    In the first video you need to move to the gameplay or action as quickly as possible. Slow theatrical productions are fine for a YouTube cinematic trailer, but it’s much more important for buyers to see what the gameplay is like.

    Requirements:

    • Size: should be of the highest resolution, up to 1920x1080 px. 16:9 (1.77) aspect ratio is preferred, but 4:3 (1.33) aspect ratio is also acceptable.

    • Format: https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUTUBE_VIDEO_ID

    Get the YouTube embed link

    1. Go to YouTube.
    2. Navigate to the video you wish to embed.
    3. Click the Share link below the video.
    1. Click the Embed link.
    1. Copy the embed link in the src attribute.

    Screenshots

    Screenshots will appear on your game’s store page as a carousel.

    Screenshots should show only the gameplay.

    Requirements:

    • Upload 5 to 7 for best results

    • Size: high-resolution widescreen screenshots in 2160x1215 px

    • Format: PNG, JPEG
